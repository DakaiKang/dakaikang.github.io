I conduct my research on **[ResilientDB](https://resilientdb.incubator.apache.org)**, a resilient data platform that centers around a democratic and decentralized computational model, with the goal of unifying secure transactional and real-time analytical processing. ResilientDB is currently under Apache Incubation.

Since 2022, alongside my talented peer Junchao, we have re-architected and re-imagined its modular system design from the ground up. Our design embeds parallelism and deep pipelining at every layer to fully harness modern hardware and cloud infrastructure globally.
