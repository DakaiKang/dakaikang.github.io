I am an **Architect** on **[ResilientDB](https://expolab.resilientdb.com)**, a resilient data platform under Apache Incubation. ResilientDB centers around a democratic and decentralized computational model, with the goal of unifying secure transactional and real-time analytical processing.

I have implemented BFT consensus protocols in ResilientDB, including **PBFT**, **HotStuff-1**, and **FairDAG**.

Since 2022, alongside my talented peer Junchao, we have re-architected and re-imagined its modular system design from the ground up. Our design embeds parallelism and deep pipelining at every layer to fully harness modern hardware and cloud infrastructure globally.
